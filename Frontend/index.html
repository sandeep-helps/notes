About Me

I am Sandeep Sharma, a Computer Science and Engineering undergraduate at Teerthanker Mahaveer University with a strong interest in data science, machine learning, and full-stack development. I enjoy building practical, real-world solutions and have worked on projects such as BloodBank+, Enggnotes, and multiple ML models for prediction and classification tasks. My technical toolkit includes Python, R, Java, SQL, C++ along with frameworks like TensorFlow, Scikit-learn, Keras and tools such as Pandas, NumPy, and Tableau. I am a national-level Smart India Hackathon 2025 Winner , where I contributed to backend development, data processing, and system design in a collaborative team environment under strict timelines. As a Data Science Intern at CodeSoft, I strengthened my skills in data preprocessing, model building, and evaluation. I am a fast learner, effective communicator, and team player passionate about AI, automation, and solving impactful problems through technology.
